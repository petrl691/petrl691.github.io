Instuctions: 1. First person (if you are not the first person move on to 2): Using the sharpie pen, draw an enclosed shape using a ruler and straight lines only. 2. Second person and so on: Using the sharpie pen, draw an enclosed shape using one side or corner of a preceding shape to form your own. This shape should be drawn using a ruler and straight lines only. 3. Using one of the colored pencils provided, shade within the confines of your shape. 4. Using the sharpie pen, write a short phrase that means something to you inside the confines of your shape. 5. Pass along to the next person. 6. Repeat the process. 7. When the last person finishes drawing their portion, the piece is finished.
